Welcome to Business

Property owners employ Caretakers to look after their property. This may mean taking care of small tasks, such as cleaning or emptying trash or running the property in the owner's absence by directing maintenance requests or making repairs. So our agency mainly connects the two parties, caretakers and property owners, while also making the process easier for the two parties. Doing background checks for the property owners and ensuring the safety of the caretakers and the property owners property.
